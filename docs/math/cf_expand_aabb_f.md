Expands an AABB (axis-aligned bounding box).

```cpp
CF_Aabb cf_expand_aabb_f(CF_Aabb aabb, float v)
```

## Remarks

`v` is added to to `max.x` and `max.y` of `aabb`, and subtracted from `min.x` and `min.y` of `aabb`.
